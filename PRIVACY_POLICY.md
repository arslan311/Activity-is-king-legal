# Privacy Policy for Activity Is King

Effective date: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Activity Is King ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Activity Is King mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Summary

Activity Is King is a local-first sales activity tracker. Most of your activity data is stored on your device. If you create an account or sign in, limited account data is used to enable authentication and subscription features. We do not sell your personal information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you provide

- Account information. If you sign up or sign in, we may collect your email address and authentication identifiers.
- Subscription information. When you purchase a subscription, we receive purchase status and entitlement data from the app store.
- Activity data. Activity logs, notes, targets, streaks, and settings you create in the App.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

- Device information. Basic device and OS information needed to run the App.
- App usage data. Minimal analytics needed to keep the app stable (if enabled).

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information to:

- Provide and maintain the App.
- Enable authentication and subscription access.
- Store and display your activity history and progress.
- Improve performance and fix issues.

## 4. Data Storage and Sync

Your activity data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable account sign-in or sync features, some data may be stored in our backend services to enable cross-device access.

## 5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data in the following cases:

- Service providers. We use providers such as Firebase for authentication and cloud services.
- Legal requirements. If required by law, we may disclose information.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. You can delete your local data by uninstalling the App. If you use account features, you may request account deletion as described below.

## 7. Your Choices

- Export. You can export your activity data to CSV from within the App.
- Delete account. You can request account deletion by contacting us.
- Disable features. You can adjust app settings in the Settings screen.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 10. International Users

If you access the App outside your country, your data may be processed in other countries where our service providers operate.

## 11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you within the App or by other means.
